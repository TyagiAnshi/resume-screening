Job Title: Senior Backend Engineer — Data Platform
Company: Meridian Analytics (Series B, ~150 employees)
Location: Remote (APAC/EMEA preferred) | Full-time

─────────────────────────────────────────────
About the Role
─────────────────────────────────────────────
We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Data Platform team.
You'll own the ingestion, transformation, and serving layer of our real-time analytics
pipeline — the backbone that processes ~5 billion events/day across 200+ enterprise customers.

This is a high-ownership role. You'll work directly with the VP of Engineering and
partner closely with ML engineers, data scientists, and product managers.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
What You'll Do
─────────────────────────────────────────────
- Design, build, and operate distributed data pipelines (Kafka, Spark, Flink) at scale
- Own the Python/Go microservices that power our ingestion and transformation APIs
- Lead architectural decisions: schema design, data modelling, system reliability
- Mentor 2-3 junior engineers; conduct code reviews and establish engineering standards
- Collaborate cross-functionally to translate business requirements into technical specs
- Drive observability: define SLOs, set up alerting, own production incidents
- Contribute to open-source tooling the team publishes (active GitHub presence a plus)

─────────────────────────────────────────────
What We're Looking For
─────────────────────────────────────────────
Must-have:
- 5+ years backend engineering, with at least 2 years on data/streaming systems
- Deep proficiency in Python; working knowledge of Go or Rust is a bonus
- Hands-on experience with streaming technologies (Kafka, Flink, Spark Streaming, or equivalent)
- Cloud platform expertise: AWS (preferred), GCP, or Azure — infrastructure-as-code (Terraform)
- Strong understanding of distributed systems concepts: CAP theorem, partitioning, fault tolerance
- Proven track record of owning and shipping production systems end-to-end

Nice-to-have:
- Experience with dbt, Airflow, or similar orchestration tools
- Familiarity with ClickHouse, Druid, or other OLAP databases
- Prior experience in a startup or high-growth engineering environment
- Open-source contributions
- Experience mentoring junior engineers

─────────────────────────────────────────────
Python 3.11 · Go 1.22 · Apache Kafka · Apache Flink · Apache Spark
PostgreSQL · ClickHouse · Redis · AWS (EKS, S3, RDS, MSK)
